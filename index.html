Feature: Return to School

Heading back to school in a pandemic with new rules, outdoor classes, and full school schedules have been challenging. But imagine navigating so many changes while also trying to go through the college application process - entirely online. All of us have had to adapt and quickly. With only two planning days to prepare, we threw ourselves into online life, and those three months forced students and teachers to look to innovative solutions, new technology, and alternative methods of understanding to get the most out of our education, all from our homes.
